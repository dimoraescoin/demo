Tokenomics of Mega Millions Coin (MMC)

Mega Millions Coin (MMC) is designed to combine the excitement of a lottery with the benefits of decentralized cryptocurrency. Here's how the tokenomics work:

Total Supply

The total supply of MMC tokens is capped at 1,000,000,000 MMC tokens, which ensures scarcity and value preservation over time.

Initial Distribution

  • 50% of tokens will be allocated for the lottery prize pool.
  • 20% will be distributed to early investors and liquidity providers.
  • 10% will go to the development team and strategic partners.
  • 10% will be reserved for marketing and community growth.
  • 5% for a charitable fund or social cause (optional).
  • 5% reserved for future treasury and governance purposes.

Lottery Prize Pool Allocation

Each lottery drawing will allocate 15% of total tokens in circulation as prize money. The prizes will be distributed as follows:

  • 1st Prize: 5% of total MMC tokens (winner can choose MMC or ETH).
  • 2nd Prize: 5% of total MMC tokens (winner can choose MMC or ETH).
  • 3rd Prize: 5% of total MMC tokens (winner can choose MMC or ETH).

Transaction Fees

Whenever MMC tokens are transferred, a small fee is applied:

  • 1% transaction fee to fund the lottery prize pool.
  • 0.5% transaction fee for community incentives and marketing activities.

Governance

MMC holders will have the power to vote on key decisions regarding the development of the project, including changes to tokenomics, future prize pool distributions, and other project-related initiatives. Governance will be conducted through a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) model.

Future Plans

In the future, we aim to integrate more cryptocurrencies into our lottery system, offer larger prize pools, and expand our global community. Stay tuned for exciting developments!
